About This Website

Hey there! I wanted to do something a little different with this portfolio. Though not too different, because let’s be honest, you’ve probably seen your fair share of sites like this.

But here’s where it stands out: I didn’t just build this site. I automated its whole deployment. One script sets up a Google Cloud environment, links it to a GitHub repo, and configures everything to auto-deploy to Cloud Run on every push. It scales to zero when idle, updates itself, and stays friendly to my wallet. If you're curious, the code’s on my GitHub.

This site is where I share my journey studying Applied Computer Science, along with my skills, certifications, and projects that reflect what I’ve been learning.

My goal? To build reliable, secure cloud systems, help people understand them, and maybe have a little fun with it along the way.

Feel free to poke around, check out my work, and say hi via the social links above!
